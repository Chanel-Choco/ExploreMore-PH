Panglao Island Tour

Be introduced to the natural and cultural wonders that make Panglao Island so popular. This half-day tour with a local guide takes in multiple island attractions. View the limestone formations at Hinagdanan Cave; learn about organic methods at a Bohol bee farm and coco farm; see the landmark Dauis Church and Panglao watchtower; find one-of-a-kind treasures at the Nova Sea Shell Museum and don’t miss the white-sand shoreline of Alona Beach.


×

Guimaras Private Day Tour from Iloilo with Lunch & Mango Pizza

You'll get to swim and relax at beautiful spots like Natago Beach, Lamurawan Island, and Ave Maria Island, and even explore the intriguing Baras Cave.

On land, the tour takes you to significant cultural sites such as the historic Guisi Lighthouse, the impressive San Lorenzo Wind Farm, and the serene Trappist Monastery, offering a glimpse into the island's rich heritage.

Philippine Eagle Journey

A visit to the Philippine Eagle Center in Davao offers an enriching experience for all. While tour guiding is free, it's recommended to book in advance to ensure a guide's availability. The center is open daily from 8 AM to 5 PM, even on holidays. Discounts are available for school groups of 30+ students and family groups of 10, provided they pre-book and pay in advance at the PEF Office.

Optional lectures cover various topics, including the Philippine Eagle Foundation's programs, conservation breeding, field research, community-based initiatives, conservation education, and captive breeding techniques.

Mt. Himaguitan Senior, Resident, Youth, Student Discounts

Mount Hamiguitan offers reduced trekking fees for several groups. Davao Oriental residents, students, and youth can pay around PHP 1,500 per person, a significant discount from the regular PHP 3,000. Senior citizens may also receive a reduced rate (e.g., PHP 2,400).
